<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C1">讲者介绍</h4>
<p>
范文芳小姐是新加坡著名演员、歌手和主持人，在她的演艺生涯中荣获多个奖项。她不仅在亚洲市场拥有知名度，还成功进军中国和好莱坞，拍摄了多部备受好评的电影，赢得了各界的肯定。她从一名成功的艺人转变为一名母亲，全心陪伴孩子成长。她为孩子营造了良好的语言学习环境，让孩子能够轻松自然地学习华文。此外，她从母亲的视角出发，亲手绘制并出版了四本绘本，展现了她对孩子无限的爱。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Profile</h4>
<p>
Ms Fann Wong is a famous Singaporean actress, singer and host who has won multiple awards throughout her acting career. After gaining popularity through films and television productions in Asia, she ventured into playing major roles in China and Hollywood productions, receiving numerous accolades worldwide. After becoming a mother, Ms Fann Wong shifted her focus to nurturing her child's holistic development. This has involved creating enriching Chinese language learning environments and crafting stories from a maternal viewpoint. Ms Fann Wong has authored and illustrated four picture books, reflecting her boundless love for her child.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C2">分享会内容简介</h4> 
<p>
父母在孩子的语言学习过程中扮演着重要的角色。范文芳小姐将分享她如何让孩子在生活中自然、轻松地学习华文。她为孩子创建有利的语言学习环境，在日常生活中用华语与孩子沟通，并通过讲故事、阅读和朗读培养孩子学习华文的兴趣。她也将分享她如何以母亲的视角，针对孩子的兴趣、爱好与好奇心，为孩子绘制了绘本。在她创作的过程中，孩子积极参与讨论，提供建议，乐在其中。亲子在温馨的氛围中互动，通过交流促进语言学习。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Synopsis</h4> 
<p>
Parents play a pivotal role in a child's language learning. Ms Fann Wong will be focusing on how she has created a conducive language learning environment in which her child can learn Chinese happily and easily. She will also share her personal experiences in creating opportunities for her child to use and learn Chinese in daily life by leveraging on her child’s interests. For instance, she will share how observing her son’s interests led to her creating children’s picture books, and how this co-creation process further developed her son’s learning and love for the language.
</p>
</div>
