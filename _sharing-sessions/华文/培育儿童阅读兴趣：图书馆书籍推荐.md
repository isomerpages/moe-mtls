<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C1">讲者介绍</h4>
<p>
翁玲月女士是新加坡国家图书馆管理局馆藏规划与发展部门的高级图书馆员。她领导一支图书馆员团队，负责挑选公共图书馆的华文儿童馆藏，为读者提供多样丰富的阅读资源。此外，她还积极参与规划和举办促进母语阅读的活动。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Profile</h4>
<p>
Lynn Ang is a Senior Librarian with the Collection &amp; Planning Development (Chinese Language) Department in National Library Board (NLB). She leads a team of librarians in the selection and development of the Chinese children's collection in Public Libraries, so as to provide readers with a diverse and enriching array of reading resources. She is also involved in planning and implementing initiatives to promote a love for reading in Mother Tongue Languages.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C2">工作坊内容简介</h4> 
<p>
儿童有不同的兴趣爱好和阅读水平，因此提供广泛的书籍选择以满足他们的喜好和符合他们的阅读能力是非常重要的。在这次分享会上，翁玲月女士将推荐图书馆里不同类型的儿童华文书籍供老师与家长们作为参考，挑选出适合孩子的书籍，增强他们的阅读参与度和乐趣。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Synopsis</h4> 
<p>
Children have diverse interests and reading levels, so it is important to offer a wide range of books to cater to their individual preferences and abilities. In this sharing session, Lynn will recommend different types of books from the library collection for teachers and parents as a reference, and help them select suitable books for children, enhancing their reading engagement and enjoyment.
</p>
</div>
