<h4 id="C1">Profile</h4>
<p>
The Mother Tongue Languages Branch (MTLB) EdTech Working Group (ETWG) is composed of curriculum officers dedicated to developing curriculum-aligned digital resources for the effective teaching and learning of Mother Tongue Languages (MTL). The group actively engages with emerging technology, leveraging tech tools and AI to customize and create tailored learning experiences that cater to diverse learning needs.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C2">Synopsis</h4> 
<p>
Parents may have observed their children expressing interest in logging in to SLS and sharing their achievements on the platform. SLS, or Student Learning Space, is a key initiative by MOE aimed at transforming students' learning experiences, promoting self-directed and collaborative learning, and enhancing their overall enjoyment of the learning process. In this session, parents will gain practical insights into the various resources and tools available on SLS, empowering them to effectively support and enrich their childâ€™s learning of MTL.
