<h4 id="C1">讲者介绍</h4>
<p>
作为本地一家媒体和内容创作公司，MCC致力于通过新颖独特的想法与形式推展富有影响力的企划项目。MCC曾与多家国内外知名品牌合作。“小胖哥哥”就是该公司支持的本地教育品牌之一。展望未来，MCC将推出更多惠及社区的寓教于乐的节目。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Profile</h4>
<p>
MCC is a media and content creation company that specializes in developing impactful projects using unique and innovative ideas and formats. The company has collaborated with various local and international IP brands, including the local educational brand Koody. Going forward, MCC is committed to producing more edutainment projects for the benefit of the community.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C2">工作坊内容简介</h4> 
<p>
小胖哥哥是一个来自遥远星球的机器人。他不断探索世界，到处结交新朋友。他拥有一种记忆共享的特殊能力，可以通过这个能力重现任何情景，让与他交流的人体验他的过去。
</p>
<p>
本次工作坊，小胖哥哥将带领孩子们进入一个充满想象力和乐趣的世界。除了聆听精彩的故事外，家长和孩子也能一起进行亲子手工活动，并和小胖哥哥一起唱唱跳跳！这个工作坊将提供一个沉浸式的体验，让小朋友跟小胖哥哥一起寻找喵喵, 通过故事、手工活动和歌曲带动唱一起学习华文。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Synopsis</h4> 
<p>
This interactive workshop brings children into a world full of imagination and fun. Together with Koody, children will embark on an adventure to search for his missing cat friend - Meow Meow. In addition to storytelling, families will take part in a parent-child handicraft activity together, and meet the character Koody from the story, singing and dancing along with him! All in all, this workshop provides a fun environment for children to be immersed in Mandarin through storytelling, hands-on activities and sing-alongs. Come join us and let's find Meow Meow together with Koody!
</p>
</div>
