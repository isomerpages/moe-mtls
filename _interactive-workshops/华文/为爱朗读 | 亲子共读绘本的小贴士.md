<h4 id="C1">讲者介绍</h4>
<p>
陈碧娟女士目前以全职妈妈和绘本推广者的身份，长期致力于推广亲子共读。她曾策划并举办多场亲子绘本阅读及文化活动，希望通过自己微薄的努力，播撒更多阅读的种子。
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Profile</h4>
<p>
Currently a full-time mother and part-time storyteller, Miss Tan Pek Kian has been actively promoting parent-child reading.
</p>
<p>
She has collaborated with the Grassroots Book Room, National Arts Council and other organizations to arrange reading and cultural activities. She has contributed actively by sowing seeds to develop the love of reading among little ones, in the hope that one day, they will blossom into avid readers who embrace reading.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4 id="C2">工作坊内容简介</h4> 
<p>
当孩子依偎在你的身边，请您给他读一本故事书的时候，您会怎么做呢？您会照本宣科还是使出浑身解数演绎一场好戏呢？或许大部分的家长会认为“陪孩子读绘本”很简单，只要指着字、照着读就没问题了吧！到底怎样阅读绘本才能达到最佳的效果呢？在共读的过程中，又如何引起孩子的共鸣，进而展开有趣的对话呢？
</p>
<p>
在这个工作坊，陈碧娟老师将跟大家分享一些阅读绘本的实用技巧，让您在往后的亲子时光里，享受共读的乐趣，一起为爱朗读！
</p>
</div>

<div class="section-wrapper">
<h4>Synopsis</h4> 
<p>
How do you respond when your child clings to you and begs you to read him/her a storybook? Do you stick to the script or do you make an effort to deliver an engaging performance?
</p>
<p>
Perhaps most parents would think that "reading a picture book" is very simple. Simply point to the words and read along and that's it! How can we achieve the best results when reading picture books? And how to strike a chord with children through reading and start interesting conversations?
</p>
<p>
Madam Tan, who has been promoting parent-child reading for a long time, will share with you some practical reading techniques. With these simple tips, you can share meaningful bonding time with your child through reading. Let's read aloud for love!
</p>
</div>
